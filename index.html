Hi, I’m Myro. Welcome to my website! I’m an independent artist, native to Saint Louis, MO. I started making music back in 2022 with my first song being named “Stackin’ Green”. I followed that single up with another one named “Kill La’ Kill” which started gaining me attention around the STL music scene. I love this, I love the art of songwriting and composing. I plan to be a platinum record selling artist in the future. I know with hardwork and consistency anything is attainable in this world, and that’s precisely what I plan to do. Execute. My family is what puts the battery in my back for me to keep pushing forward. Even though there are times when I may get down or discouraged, I always remember what and who I’m doing it for. Your Support Means The World, Thank You!
